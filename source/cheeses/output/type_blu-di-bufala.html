Blu di Bufala

Blu di Bufala is an Italian blue-veined cheese made with full-fat pasteurized buffalo milk by Quattro Portoni in Bergamo, Italy.

It has a square shape and dry and wrinkly rind with an amber grey colour. The texture of the paste ranges from creamy to crumbly with a light cream colour and blue veins spread throughout. The aroma has a milky, acidic note while the flavour is distinct, slightly intense with the sweetness of buffalo's milk.

  • Country of origin: Italy
  • Region: Lombardy
  • Family: Blue
  • Type: semi-hard , artisan, blue-veined
  • Texture: creamy and crumbly
  • Rind: natural
  • Colour: cream
  • Flavour: acidic, milky, sweet, tangy
  • Aroma: fresh, milky
  • Vegetarian: no
  • Producers: Azienda Agricola Gritti Bruno E Alfio S.s. Societa Agricola
