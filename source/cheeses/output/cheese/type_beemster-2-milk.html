Beemster 2% Milk

Beemster 2% Milk is made using 2% milk and has less fat than other Dutch aged cheeses. It is a healthier choice with great flavour but reduced fat. Enjoy the nutty taste and fresh floral aroma of the cheese with salads, tacos, and sandwiches. The cheese is l actose & gluten free and boasts a high content of calcium (29%).

  • Made from pasteurized cow 's milk
  • Country of origin: Canada , Denmark , France , Germany , Holland and United States
  • Type: semi-soft
  • Fat content (in dry matter): 8%
  • Fat content: 5 g/100g
  • Texture: smooth
  • Flavour: nutty
  • Aroma: aromatic, floral, fruity
  • Vegetarian: no
  • Producers: Beemster Cheese, CONO Kaasmakers / Beemster
