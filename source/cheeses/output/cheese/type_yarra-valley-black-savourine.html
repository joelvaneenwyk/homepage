Yarra Valley Black Savourine

Black Savourine is a semi-matured fresh goat's milk cheese from Yarra Valley Dairy located 50 kilometers from Melbourne, Victoria in Australia. A cheese in the shape of a pyramid with a flat top is coated with ash has acidic and sour taste. This firm, little moist textured cheese has roasted nuts flavour with hints of blue, cooked cream. Black Savourine goes well with Quince paste, fruit bread, dry fruits.

A great accompaniment for Sangiovese, Pinot Noir, Tempranillo.

  • Made from goat 's milk
  • Country of origin: Australia
  • Region: Yarra Valley, Victoria
  • Type: semi-hard , artisan
  • Fat content: 22.5 g/100g
  • Texture: firm
  • Rind: ash coated
  • Colour: white
  • Flavour: acidic, sour
  • Aroma: fresh, nutty
  • Vegetarian: yes
  • Producers: Yarra Valley Dairy
