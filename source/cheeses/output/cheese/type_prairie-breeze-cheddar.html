Prairie Breeze Cheddar

Prairie Breeze is a well-aged white Cheddar style cheese and aged for a minimum of 9 months. It is made with vegetarian rennet with no added colour. The texture is crumbly yet creamy with a slight crunch from the calcium crystals developed during the prolonged aging. Flavours are medium sharp with some subtle grassy tones.

  • Made from pasteurized cow 's milk
  • Country of origin: United States
  • Region: Iowa
  • Family: Cheddar
  • Type: hard , artisan
  • Texture: creamy , crumbly and grainy
  • Rind: natural
  • Colour: yellow
  • Flavour: nutty, sharp, sweet
  • Aroma: grassy, mild
  • Vegetarian: yes
  • Producers: Milton Creamery LLC
