Champignon de Luxe Garlic

Champignon de Luxe Garlic is a soft-ripened, double-cream cheese made by Käserei Champignon, Lauben / Allgäu, Germany. This Brie style cheese has herbal and spicy flavour. Finely chopped chives and garlic gives a distinction colour and aroma. The cheese can be eaten with steak, bread or meal. It pairs well with strong white wines or  red Bordeaux wines.

  • Made from pasteurized cow 's milk
  • Country of origin: Germany
  • Region: Allgäu
  • Type: soft , soft-ripened
  • Texture: creamy
  • Rind: natural
  • Colour: cream
  • Flavour: garlicky, herbaceous
  • Aroma: herbal, spicy
  • Vegetarian: yes
  • Producers: Käserei Champignon
  • Synonyms: Champignon Garlic, Champignon de Luxe Knoblauch
