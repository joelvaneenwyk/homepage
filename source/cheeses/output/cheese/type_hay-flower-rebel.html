Hay Flower Rebel

Hay Flower Rebel Cheese is a semi-hard, creamy cheese from Käserebellen, processed only from the purest hay-milk of cows that are fed on dried hay, herbs and grains of corn and not on industry concentrates. This "allergy friendly" cheese is 100% GMO-free and without fermented foodstuffs. It's got a great rich flavour of flower meadow from the hay-milk with lingering scents of honey, floral aromas and young wood.

This celebrated cheese has won bronze award at the 2014 World Cheese Awards.

  • Made from unpasteurized cow 's milk
  • Country of origin: Austria
  • Region: Sulzberg
  • Type: semi-hard , artisan
  • Fat content: 50%
  • Texture: creamy and firm
  • Rind: natural
  • Colour: golden yellow
  • Flavour: creamy, grassy
  • Aroma: floral, woody
  • Producers: Sulzberger Käserebellen Sennerei GmbH
  • Synonyms: Heublumenrebell
